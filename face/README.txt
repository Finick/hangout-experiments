== Hangout Apps Sample - 3d Head Hangout App ==

This is a demo that shows how to use three.js and
hangouts together.  The head will move with your head;
the mouth will move with the volume from your mic.

== What's Included ==

3dHead.xml:  This file comprises the entire hangout app.
